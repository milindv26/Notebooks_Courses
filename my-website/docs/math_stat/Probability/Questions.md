# Questions

1. Three distinct numbers are selected from 100 natural number. What is the probability that all the three numbers are divisible by 2 and 3?
2. A divisor of 2700 is selected. What is the probability that it is a multiple of 4 but not 5?
3. Robot is 3 cm from the left of the table and 17cm from the right of the table. The robot moves with equal probability 10cm to the left or to the right. What is the average number of steps for the robot to fall of off the table?
4. You and your friend are playing a game with a fair coin. The two of you will continue to toss the coin until the sequence HH or TH shows up. If HH shows up first, you win, and if TH shows up first your friend win. What is the probability of you winning the game?
5. The letters of the word 'ASSASSIN' are written down at random in a row. What is the probability that no two 'S' occur together?
Out of 21 tickets market with numbers from 1 to 21, three are drawn at random. What is the probability that the numbers form an Arithmetic Progression?
